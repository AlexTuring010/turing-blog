Three assignments, one semester, enough segfaults for a lifetime.

## What it was

The assignment series in the OS course asked us to write *underneath* the user-space we knew. Not to call threads — to build them. Not to look at virtual memory — to write the page-replacement algorithm. Not to use a filesystem — to expose one.

Each has its own case study.
